AICB boys and girls Hostel :

AICB hostel is situated in the AICB campus. It is only for the trainees of AICB. Separate rooms for students of different courses are allotted so that they can discus with eachother and learn their respective course well. There are separate sections for girls and boys. Hygenic, healthy, north-Indian vegetarian food is provided to the hostellites. The campus contains a beautiful, well-maintained garden as well.
